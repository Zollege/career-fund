Our Passion Is Education

We believe that education is the key to successful, meaningful lives. But for too many, it's out of reach. We created Career Fund to make education accessible to everyone.

What We Believe

Our Partners are our highest priority, which is why we created a simple, intuitive platform that puts the power back in the hands of students they serve, enabling them to create a payment plan that starts them on their career education the same day.

Empowerment

Put the power in our students hands to create a plan that meets their unique financial needs

Transparency

No hidden fees, no deceptive language or complex terms

Protection

We combine fair financing options with the most advanced security technology to ensure our students and partners are protected
