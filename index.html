Our Mission

Our mission is to make education that can lead to fulfilling careers accessible to everyone. We do this by partnering with select Trade Schools to empower students to select a payment plan that fits their financial situation. We believe in transparency which is why we have no hidden fees, avoid deceptive language and complex financial terms. We combine fair financing options with the most advanced security technology to ensure our students and partners are protected.
